The carefully tuned REQ-030 scatter material (PIN_PHYSICS friction 0.55, restitution 0) and the belly-heavy mass are deliberately UNCHANGED. The down-lane visual roll is essentially unchanged (the ball sits on the bed either way and bed friction is low enough that the launch spin barely moves the visible roll). The cord tether was investigated and ruled out (it stayed slack at impact; REQ-014).
