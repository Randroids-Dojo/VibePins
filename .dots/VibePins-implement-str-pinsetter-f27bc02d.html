implement: String pinsetter tether (slack overhead cord per pin)

Tether each of the 10 pins at the neck to a fixed overhead anchor above its home spot via a length-limited Rapier joint (REQ-013, REQ-015). Slack so pins fall/slide/collide freely on impact (REQ-014); cord only goes taut when lifted or dragged far. Render the cords. Detection (REQ-016/017) and reset cycle (REQ-018/019) are separate later slices.
