implement: Pin standing/fallen detection with a settle window

After the ball and pins settle, classify each pin as standing (up-axis upright within tolerance AND at rest in linear+angular velocity AND still on the deck footprint) or fallen (REQ-016). A settle window waits for motion to die down before counting so wobbling pins are not miscounted (REQ-017). Pure classifier + a detector over the PinSet; feeds scoring/pin-accounting later. Reset cycle (REQ-018/019) and scoring wiring are separate slices.
